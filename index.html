PAWBOOK Case Study

Overview

PAWBOOK is a social platform designed for pet owners to connect, share updates about their pets, and manage pet profiles. The platform aims to create a community where users can exchange tips, showcase pet photos, and keep track of pet-related activities.

Problem Statement

Many pet owners struggle to find a dedicated online space for connecting with other pet enthusiasts. Existing social media platforms are general-purpose, lacking features specifically for pets and their owners.

Objectives

  • Build a user-friendly platform for pet owners to interact.
  • Create an intuitive UI/UX for profile management and posting.
  • Implement messaging, notifications, and community engagement.
  • Design a responsive platform for desktop and mobile users.

Design & Development (In Progress)

High-fidelity designs are created in Figma. Pages for home, pet profiles, chat, notifications, and posts are under development. Working on integrating smooth animations, responsive layouts, and interactive elements.

Current Status & Future Plans

Project is actively being developed; core frontend structure is in place. Upcoming features include backend integration for real-time messaging, post management, and community groups. Future improvements include AI-based content suggestions and enhanced community engagement tools.
